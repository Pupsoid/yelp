Quirky Reviews
The odds are better
This project presents a fresh perspective on Yelp reviews, the quirky ones. We set out to see if the outlying reviews were different (better) than the average reviews in any way. Our experiments reveal that the outlying reviews received many more useful and funny votes than a regular review. The following tool enables one to locate businesses that received such outlying reviews.
